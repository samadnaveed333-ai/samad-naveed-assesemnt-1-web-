Our Story

How It All Started

Founded in 2018, Cocoa & Co. grew from one obsession: making the perfect chocolate chip cookie. What began in a tiny flat kitchen in Edinburgh — with one mixing bowl, a hand-me-down oven, and far too much butter — quickly became something much bigger than we ever imagined.

Word spread fast. Neighbours started knocking on our door, colleagues started placing weekly orders, and before long, we realised we weren't just baking cookies — we were baking joy. In 2020, we opened our first proper bakery on the high street, and the queue on opening morning stretched around the block. That told us everything we needed to know.

What Makes Us Different?

At Cocoa & Co., we believe a truly great cookie starts with truly great ingredients. We use only free-range eggs, Belgian dark chocolate, and locally sourced butter. No artificial preservatives, no shortcuts, no compromises. Every batch is hand-rolled, baked to order, and cooled on the rack — exactly the way your grandmother would have done it.

🔥 Our Secret Ingredient

Wondering what makes our cookies so impossibly good? It's all in the chill. We rest every batch of dough in the fridge for a full 24 hours before baking. This slow cold fermentation deepens the flavour, enhances the caramel notes in the brown sugar, and gives our cookies that signature crispy edge with an irresistibly soft, gooey centre. Patience, it turns out, is a flavour.

Looking Ahead

We're not slowing down. In 2025, we launched our nationwide delivery service so that no one in the UK has to go without a warm Cocoa & Co. cookie. We're expanding our range too — think salted caramel brownies, double-fudge blondies, and a seasonal menu that changes with the best ingredients of each month.

But no matter how much we grow, one thing will never change: every cookie we make is still shaped by the same love and obsession that started it all in that tiny Edinburgh kitchen. Welcome to Cocoa & Co. — baked with joy, eaten with happiness.
